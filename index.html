Controls:

{Name of key} = Thing that happens when you press that key

{Space} = Play through poem linearly

{qwerty...xcvb...1234...} = Play lines individually in any order

{Escape} = Go to Table of Contents

{Right Arrow} = Go to next piece

{Left Arrow} = Go to previous piece

{Down Arrow} = Show/hide controls for individual piece

{Ctrl + R} = Restart Piece

{Ctrl + Q} = Quit

These options can also be accessed in the menu by moving your mouse to the top of the screen

Press {Escape} to go to the Table of Contents
