Yardley, PA 1997 - Guide

Wait a bit at startup because there's a lot of audio to load

Press F11 for Full Screen

Press keys in this order, pressing the next key when the audio from the previous key stops

q w e r t y u i o p [ ]

a s d f g h j k l ;

z x c v b n m , ./

1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 0

Then we get to the 'bridge': where you can press keys multiple times and fill up the screen. I also like pressing two keys at once, lots of room to improvise and find hidden melodies / textures

When you're ready to end press Shift +q, Shift +w, Shift +e, then Shift +r for the ending sequence.



These are just suggestions though, the real secret is that you can play it however you want
